⚡ Rules to stay on track with 30–60 min/day

  • Never skip two days in a row — consistency beats intensity every time
  • Use your commute or lunch for watching tutorials; evenings for hands-on coding
  • Every week: write 1 sentence summarising what you learned. Forces retention
  • Use Claude to explain anything confusing instantly — don't lose momentum googling
  • Apply to roles from Week 12 onwards — don't wait until you feel "ready"
  • Tell people at work what you're learning — creates accountability and visibility
